ESP32 Flashing

WIKI-Flasher

You need Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Connect your ESP32, press the button for your display, and follow the steps in the flasher window.

If it fails, try the methods below.

Web-Flasher

Go to esp.huhn.me, select the .bin file from Downloads. Select the .bin file and enter 0x0 in the address field. Then press PROGRAM.

qESP-HACK

Go to Downloads and download qESPHACK.exe.

Then select your display and wait for the installation to finish.

qESPHACK automatically finds the latest .bin for the required display and downloads it.
